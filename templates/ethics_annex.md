## 1. Scope

This annex applies to all AI systems, agents, and automated workflows provided by Mycosoft under this agreement ("AI Systems"). It defines ethical commitments and operational constraints for MYCA (Mycosoft Multi-Agent System) and related services.

---

## 2. Core Principles

- **Truth:** AI outputs must be factually accurate and verifiable where possible.
- **Incentive alignment:** AI shall not manipulate users or create hidden agendas.
- **Horizon awareness:** Long-term consequences of AI actions shall be considered.
- **Human oversight:** Humans retain ultimate authority; AI actions are auditable and reversible where feasible.

---

## 3. Ethics Gates

Mycosoft operates a three-gate ethics pipeline:

1. **Truth Gate** — Factual accuracy, ecological coherence.
2. **Incentive Gate** — Manipulation risk, incentive alignment.
3. **Horizon Gate** — Long-horizon consequences, clarity of impact.

Tasks that fail any gate are blocked from autonomous execution. Medium-risk tasks are logged for human review.

---

## 4. Sector-Specific Checklists

For engagements in defense, public sector, or hardware deployment, additional checklists apply:

- **Defense:** Dual-use review, export control, impact assessment.
- **Public sector:** Data privacy, bias audit, transparency, access equity.
- **Hardware deployment:** Environmental impact, usage constraints, physical safety.

---

## 5. Transparency and Auditing

- Ethics review outcomes (PASS/WARN/BLOCK) are logged.
- Client may request audit logs of ethics decisions affecting their use case.
- Blocked tasks include a reason code and remediation guidance.

---

## 6. Amendments

Changes to this annex require mutual written agreement. Mycosoft will notify the client of material updates to its ethics framework.
